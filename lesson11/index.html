Be Aware of Wind Chill

During the wintertime when temperatures drop into the single digits, it's important to remember how much colder it can feel when the wind starts blowing. Winds can increase the effect of cold air on our bodies, and we can face the risk of hypothermia or even frost bite in a very short amount of time. As you'll see from this chart, wind chill is a factor even in non-freezing temperatures. Be prepared for surprisingly cold weather or an unexpected overnight stay on the side of the road. Always keep emergency blankets and other warming items in your vehicle, just in case you or someone else needs to stay warm.

Target Audience

Description

Providing weather forecasts, events, announcements, and visitor info.

Personas

1) Ice fisher checking the weather to see if the lakes are likely to be frozen thick enough to walk on, build a fire on, and camp out for a few days at a time, cooking, eating, and partying with friends who enjoy the rough outdoor experience under the stars. Also check local events to see when the ice fishing festival occurs, and checking traffic reports in case there are construction or weather delays for travelers.

2) Kite flying hobbyist who looks for activities with kite clubs, and checks the forecast to know when good flying conditions are predicted. It's best when windy to get the kite up, but also overcast so nobody has to gaze directly at the sun while watching their kite, but also with no likelyhood of lightning or precipitation, for safety and convenience.

Scenarios

1) Public safety: Alerts for floods, heavy snow, high wind, tsunami, volcano, low flying aircraft.

2) City planning: Notices for construction schedule impacting roadway commutes.

3) Event planning: Community outreach center activities.

4) Utility updates: Status of power outtages and restorations.

5) Visitor information: Highlights of sight seeing locations and schedules for tourists.
